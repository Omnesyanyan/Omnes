Haus der Musik - Vienna

The Haus der Musik Vienna offers an exciting exploration of the world of music and sound. Set in the house where Otto Nicolai, founder of the Vienna Philharmonic, lived around 150 years ago, this interactive museum aims to encourage its visitors to be open-minded about music and sound. From street sounds to music by the great composers, the Haus der Musik explores it all.
